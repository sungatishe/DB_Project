NASA

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (Eng. National Aeronautics and Space Administration, abbr. NASA (NASA)) is an independent agency belonging to the US federal government and reporting directly to the US President. It carries out the civil space program of the country, as well as scientific research of air and outer space and scientific and technological research in the field of aviation, aeronautics and astronautics (according to the terminology adopted in the United States - astronautics).
